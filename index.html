About Me

I am a Junior Undergraduate at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur majoring in Computer Science and Engineering, broadly interested in the areas of Probabilistic Machine Learning and Deep Learning.

My sophomore year research work has been centered around using Tensors for Approximate Inference on multi-way and temporal data. Under the supervision of Prof. Ketan Rajawat, I developed a Tensor based model using First Order Auto Regressive Time Series for solving the problem of Traffic Estimation and Prediction.

I started working with Prof.Soumendu Sundar Mukherjee in December 2019 at Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. We have been working on identifying Change Points in textual data such as topic changes in the news articles over the years and writing style changes across different eras.

This Semester(Jan20-Apr20) I have registered for EE609A(Convex Optimization in Signal Processing and Communications) and MTH535(An Introduction to Bayesian Analysis) and am very excited to explore and study these topics.

Last summer I explored Probabilistic Machine Learning. I studied the preliminaries of Bayesian Inference, Non-Conjugacy and Conditional Conjugacy, Linear Models and Exponential Families, Latent Variable Models, EM and VI Algorithms, and Markov Chain Monte Carlo. I worked on Recommender Systems using Bayesian Matrix Factorisation and Poisson Matrix Factorisation. I also explored Variational Autoencoders.

In my Freshmen Year, I was fascinated by Deep Learning. After learning the basics of Deep Learning, I spent quite some time exploring Generative Adversarial Networks.

I’ve had some exposure to Natural Language Processing, though I’m much less acquainted with the subject that I’d like to be. I hope to explore NLP rigorously in the future.

Traffic Prediction using Variational Bayesian Inference for Tensor Factorisation and Completion

Studied and understood paper on Variational Bayesian Inference for Robust Streaming Tensor Factorization and Completion by Cole Hawkins and Zheng Zhang. Explored avenues of Tensor Algebra, Low-rank Tensor Factorization and Time Series Forecasting. Studied and understood paper on Traffic Estimation via Online Variational Bayesian Subspace Filtering by Ketan Rajawat. Extended the findings in the above two papers by deriving an expression such that Tensor Factorization and Completion follows first order auto regressive model for its temporal component.

Probabilistic Machine Learning on GMMs,VAEs and Recommender Systems
