About Me

I am a Senior Undergraduate at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur majoring in Computer Science and Engineering, broadly interested in the areas of Large Scale Multi Agent Reinforcement Learning, Behavioral Models and Game Theory, Decision Making under Uncertainty, Generative Models.

Currently I am a Research Assistant at CARE.AI Lab at Singapore Management University working with Prof.Arunesh Sinha, Prof. Pradeep Varakantham and Prof. Main Ahn Tien.

Previously I have worked with Prof.Soumendu Sundar Mukherjee (December 2019, May 2020-October 2020) at Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata and with Prof.Piyush Rai at IIT Kanpur (December 2020-March 2021). In my sophomore year, I worked with Prof. Ketan Rajawat at IIT Kanpur.

For recreation purposes, I play guitar and am an avid follower of Formula1.
