About Me

I am currently on the industry job market! Here is my CV and research overview.

I am a PhD candidate at Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Washington, coadvised by Prof. Maryam Fazel and Prof. Simon S. Du. Since September 2024, I am a Visiting Research Scientist at Meta Superintelligence Labs FAIR, working with Lin Xiao.

My research centers on building AI systems for diverse users, developing post-training methods, evaluation benchmarks, and theoretical foundations across:

  • Few-shot personalization from memory
  • Inference-time preference elicitation
  • AI safety and robustness
  • Mechanism design for multi-agent systems

See my Google Scholar for a complete list of papers, including publications at ICLR, NeurIPS, COLM, AISTATS, Oral at AAAI, IJCAI, and ECAI.

Prior to UW, I was a Research Engineer at Singapore Management University working with Prof. Arunesh Sinha and Prof. Pradeep Varakantham. I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Kanpur in 2021, where I was advised by Prof. Piyush Rai and Prof. Ketan Rajawat.
